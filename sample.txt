Feel-good qualities of natural woodThe pieces in the exhibit were donated to the museum by collectors Fleur Bresler and her late husband , Charles . Bresler says her love of wood art began in this same gallery when she saw a similar exhibit decades earlier . If there hadn't been guards on duty, says Bresler, she "probably would have taken the tops off the cases, picked them things up and touched them.""It is alive because it comes from something that is living. And you can pick it up, you can hold it, you can feel it," she says.Bresler was further inspired by the wide range of colors that she discovered while collecting her wood art. "I had never seen wood in that many colors with that many patterns in it," she says. " I was hooked . "
